Why Green IT events matter

Sustainability-focused technology conferences offer a rare opportunity to step back from hype and reflect on the real environmental and social costs of digital systems.

In this article, I’m sharing a few reflections from recent Green IT events and how they shaped my thinking around responsible technology.

Key themes and observations

Across conferences like GreenIO, Apidays, and GenerationAI, several recurring themes emerged: energy efficiency, system boundaries, and the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration.

Many talks highlighted that optimisation alone is not enough — we also need cultural and organisational change to reduce overall impact.

What I’m taking forward

These events reinforced my commitment to lightweight, transparent, and maintainable systems. Sustainability is not a feature to bolt on, but a design constraint to embrace from the start.
